Lemon Teriyaki Chicken

Description

description goes here

Ingredients

  • list of ingredients goes here
  • 1/4 cup of lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1, 1/2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of water
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic
  • 1/2 tablespood of ground ginger
  • 4 chicken thighs or breasts

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients except chicken.
  2. Cook over medium heat for 3 to 4 minutes. DO NOT OVERCOOK!
  3. Add chicken, simmer 30 minutes for entire breast, 10 to 15 minutes for tenders, 20 minutes for thighs.
  4. Remove chicken. Add 1 tablespoon of cornstarch and cook until thick.
  5. Put chicken back in and stir until coated in sauce.
