Oriental Fried Rice

Description

description goes here

Ingredients

  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • 2 slices of bacon, cut into 1/4 inch pieces
  • 1 tablespoon of bacon drippings
  • 1, 1/2 tablespood of chopped onion
  • 1, 1/2 cups of cooked rice
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of sliced green onion
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of pepper

Steps

  1. In a medium skillet, scramble eggs then set them aside.
  2. In the same skillet, fry bacon until cooked but not crispy.
  3. In same skillet saute onions in bacon drippings until clear.
  4. Add rice, cook over medium heat until hot.
  5. Add soy sauce, salt, pepper and green onions; stir to combine.
  6. Add scrambled eggs and bacon.
