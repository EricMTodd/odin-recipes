Pasta Salad

A vibrant, flavor-packed Pasta Salad that's perfect for picnics, potlucks, or a refreshing weeknight side! Tossed with a medley of crunchy vegetables, salty feta cheese, savory pepperoni/salami, and a tangy Italian vinaigrette. This quick and customizable classic is ready in minutes and gets even better as the flavors meld overnight.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz of rotini pasta
  • 10 oz grape or cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 of a red onion
  • 1/2 of a cucumber
  • 2 bell peppers
  • Kalamata Olives
  • Sliced pepporcinis or banana peppers
  • Pepperoni and or salami
  • Feta Cheese
  • Italian, lemon or balsamic vinegarette
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Garlic powder to taste

Steps

  1. Chop veggies while pasta water boils
  2. Boil pasta for 6 minutes
  3. Drain pasta and rinse with cold water
  4. Combine all ingredients in a bowl to desired variety and taste
