Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Chicken
  • 1 Tsp Kosher Salt
  • 1 Tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 Tsp Onion Powder
  • 1 Tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 Tsp Dried Thyme
  • 1/2 Tsp Dried Rosemary
  • 1/4 Tsp Cayenne Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat an oven to 400 degrees
  2. Pat the chicken dry with paper towels and rub down with the spice blend
  3. Place the chicken in a cast iron skillet and roast for 90 minutes
  4. Pull the chicken out of the oven and let stand for 5 to 15 minutes
  5. Carve and serve
